Tokenize it

Name it, mint it. On the real thing this is Privy auth + Arweave upload + a Solana mint — here every step is simulated so you can feel the flow.

  • Encoding audio & metadata
  • Uploading to Arweave — permanent storage (simulated)
  • Minting on Solana (simulated)
  • Royalties set — you earn on every future trade
